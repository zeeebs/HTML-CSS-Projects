Steps To Make A Nut Butter & Jelly Sandwhich:

  1. Get ingredients: bread, nut butter, jelly.
  2. Spread nut butter on one piece of bread and jelly on the other.
  3. Combine both pieces.

Types of Bread:

  • Sourdough
  • Wheat
  • White

Types of Nut Butter:

  • Peanut
  • Almond
  • Cashew

Types of Jelly:

  • Strawberry
  • Boysenberry
  • Apricot
